Fruits and Vegetables are the main need of day-to-day life of humans. As food is a basic need of humans the fruits and vegetables play a major role in the food need. In present day customers usually go to a supermarket, a weekly fair or another shop to fully fill their fruits and vegetable needs. As the majority goes to the supermarket the current system has given them a lot of problems regarding the prices of fruits and Vegetables.

The large problem is the un-awareness of the customers about the prices of fruits and vegetables. Many customers face problems when they go to a shop to buy fruits and vegetables without knowing the prices or without making a budget for their next week’s fruits and vegetables. In this sector there are many applications to check the current price of fruits, but there aren’t many applications to check the predicted price of fruits and vegetables before customers go to the supermarket.

The suggested solution FruGe, is a system with a full dashboard and an interactive website to explore and analyze the past prices of fruits, vegetables and predict and maintain the future price of fruits and vegetables and present this procedure in a user-friendly way for the customers and users
